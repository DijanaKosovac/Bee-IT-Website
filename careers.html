The benefits of working with Bee IT

When you are surrounded by open-minded and communicative people, tackling tech problems is not hard, but FUN. Our team is small but carefully selected. Join our beehive and grow with us.

Remote work

We want you to have comfort in work. Our relationships are based on trust, and we give you that trust so you can manage your work assignments as you see fit.

Career empowerment

We invest in our people. You get to work with people who are eager to share their skills and are hungry for knowledge.

Healthy body & mind

A healthy bee is a happy bee. We care about your health and each of our team members gets paid gym or crossfit membership and private physicians.

Knowledge sharing community

We support the lifestyle of continuous learning and allocate certain numbers of working hours for your education and career growth.

Work-life balance

It’s important we lead a harmonious lifestyle. You get 20 vacation days to renew your energy. Certainly, you get to organize your holidays the way you prefer.

A special perk

A jar of honey a month. We all know honey is good for our body and mind. You will work with people who do work hard, but laugh harder and have a great sense of humor :)
